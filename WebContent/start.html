Welcome to ConfiLog

Welcome to the ConfiLog project's website. The primary goal of ConfiLog is to put Software Product Lines research into practice through the guidance of practitioners in the field during the configuration process. With ConfiLog you can also edit, share and download product line models instantly.

ConfiLog is working on the following Internet browsers (Windows): Safari, Mozilla, Google Chrome and Microsoft IE. We value your feedback a lot!
